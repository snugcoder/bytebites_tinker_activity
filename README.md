##Core Concepts##

- Students must understand how to use Copilot to aid in the architecture of backend OOP design. Collaborating this way would allow students to gain a deeper understanding of OOP and utilize those skill sto build foundational intelligent website architecture. 

----
##Where Students May Struggle##

- Students may struggle with understanding Copilot translation from image to the file it produces and with patience in understanding the depth behind Copilots reasoning and system design in itself. But patience and curiosity will allow students to flourish. 


----
##Where AI was helpful v. meisleading##
- AI was helpful in understanding where to begin when it came to thinking in a direction of System Design as well as providing scale instantly. 
- AI maybe misleading in providing specific and often constraining solution to Class definitions, such as providing a calculateCost + a getTotalCost in the same UML class category for Transaction. It can a little bit too literal which drive a different perspective in system design. 


----
##Student Guidance w/o Giving the Answer##
- Think deeply before asking your questions to Copilot. Because your prompting can quickly change Copilots guidance into authority over the System Design over your project. It is important to think about how Copilot can provide a robust solution to an already established System Design direction.
